How to participate in Food Portrait Project

You can search content by age, gender, city, date, etc. (demographic portrait)

You can also Log in or Sign up to:

  • Complete the user profile questionnaire
  • Upload your meal photos: a photo of one meal, or every meal for a week, a month, or more.
  • Upload photo of one meal, or every meal for a week, a month, special occasion, or just meals you eat out or meals you make.
    If you can take the photos from straight above and include the entire plate then that will create a consistency for broader groups photos.
  • Create a portrait grid of your group or yourself through what you eat. Keep uploading, and watch how your portrait evolves over time.
